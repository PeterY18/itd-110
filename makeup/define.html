Bubble Tea Definitions

Bubble Tea
Bubble tea is flavored tea that has boba. I use the term lightly and I may be referring to the smoothie variant.
Boba
Boba is the black pearl things in standard bubble teas. They are made out of tapioca and have a nice chewy texture.
Toppings
Bubble tea no longer exclusively requires boba to be bubble tea. Other toppings can be used such as jelly or special bobas
Ice Chunks
Ice chunks are the worst. They are tiny pieces of ice that ruin a bubble tea. They are made most likely from a weak blender.
