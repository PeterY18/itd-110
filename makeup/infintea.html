Infini-Tea

Infinitea is one of my latest finds for bubble tea. I have passed and missed it so many times. The first time I went there was great. The cafe serves bubble tea and other food. I haven't tried their food but there is bubble tea smoothies are great. I found no ice chunks and it was blended extremely smooth.

They also a great variety of toppings such as the popping boba, crystal boba, and a variety of jellies. I highly recommend the lychee jelly. The jellies are chewy and flavorful.

The cafe is clean and stylish with an acceptable amount of seats. The staff was fine and good straws were provided. Overall, this is what bubble tea places should strive to be.
