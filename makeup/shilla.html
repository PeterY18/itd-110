Shilla Bakery

I have known about Shilla Bakery for so long. It is a great cafe with many pastie breads. I think the cafe's bread and other dishes overshadow the bubble tea. I will probably do some bread reviews after I'm done with the bubble reviews.

The bubble teas are great. They come with good boba and a variety of flavors. The honeydew melon flavor is the best. However, Shilla Bakery slides to a second place because I have found some ice chunks. Not always, but enough to notice it.

The atmosphere Shilla Bakery provides is better than Infini-Tea. There is plenty of room and lots of samples! I have tried many breads and my favorite is the strawberry cream and jalapeno bread. I also like this toasted pizza looking thing that I don't know what is called.
