Why Everyone Should Install Arch Once In Their Life

Arch is an operating system like windows and macOS. It is a specific distrubtion under linux. It is often known for a less than friendly community and difficult installations. The difficult installations seems daunting but the benefits are great. Here are some other reasons to use Arch linux.

  • Arch is highly modular
